Personal Portfolio Site Plan

Site Name

Name: My Digital Portfolio

Reason: This name represents a personal portfolio that showcases my experience and skills in digital marketing and web development.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a professional online presence for job applications and interviews. It will showcase my projects, work experience, education, and provide a way for potential employers to contact me.

Scenarios

  • What marketing campaigns have I worked on?
  • How can I contact this person for a job opportunity?
